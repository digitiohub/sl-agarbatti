About Us

Welcome to the fragrant world of SL Agarbatti, founded in 1996 by Rajesh Vishwanathrao Sonkar. Man with great Vision and Compassion. Many customers have highly appreciated our wide range of Agarbatti fragrances. We always maintain optimum quality and sell at reasonable prices. We are an experienced firm and have been sincerely serving the industry for more than two decades.

Our company is based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. SL Agarbatti was founded in 1996, by Mr Rajesh Sonkar In Mumbai, India, and has grown to become a trusted name in Agarbatti Industry. Mr Rajesh Sonkar with his passion for fragrances and deep-rooted family traditions in Agarbatti manufacturing, set out to create a company that would blend traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. From our modest beginnings in a small workshop, SL Agarbatti has expanded its operations across the country, catering to a diverse clientele that appreciates the rich aromas and spiritual significance of our products. Over the years, we have stayed true to our founder's vision of delivering high-quality Agarbatti that enhances rituals, meditation, and everyday moments of tranquillity.

Uniquely Aromatic, Eternally Divine

Premium Quality:Our incense sticks are crafted from the finest raw materials, ensuring a soothing and long-lasting fragrance that elevates any space.

Eco-Friendly Practices:We are committed to sustainability, employing eco-friendly manufacturing processes and sourcing natural ingredients to minimize our environmental impact.

Innovative Scents:Our dedicated R&D team continuously develops new and unique fragrances, keeping our product line fresh and appealing to a broad range of customers.

Customer Satisfaction: With a 95% customer satisfaction rate, we prioritize your experience by delivering exceptional products and responsive service.

Global Reach: Having expanded into international markets, including Europe, South Africa, Malaysia, and the USA, we bring our exquisite fragrances to homes worldwide.
