About Us

Company Desk

At SL Agarbatti, we blend traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to create incense sticks that enhance your spiritual practices and everyday moments. Based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, we've been dedicated to delivering high-quality products for over two decades. Our team of skilled artisans and professionals work tirelessly to ensure each incense stick embodies excellence and purity.

Founder Desk

Welcome to the fragrant world of SL Agarbatti, founded in 1996 by Rajesh Vishwanathrao Sonkar. Man with great Vision and Compassion. Many customers have highly appreciated our wide range of Agarbatti fragrances. We always maintain optimum quality and sell at reasonable prices. We are an experienced firm and have sincerely served the industry for over two decades.

Our company is based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. SL Agarbatti was founded in 1996, by Mr Rajesh Sonkar in Mumbai, India, and has grown to become a trusted name in Agarbatti Industry. Mr Rajesh Sonkar with his passion for fragrances and deep-rooted family traditions in Agarbatti manufacturing, set out to create a company that would blend traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. From our modest beginnings in a small workshop, SL Agarbatti has expanded its operations across the country, catering to a diverse clientele that appreciates the rich aromas and spiritual significance of our products. Over the years, we have stayed true to our founder's vision of delivering high-quality Agarbatti that enhances rituals, meditation, and everyday moments of tranquility.

The Legacy of Fragrance

SL Agarbatti was founded in 1996, by Mr Rajesh Sonkar In Mumbai, India, and has grown to become a trusted name in Agarbatti Industry. Mr Rajesh Sonkar with his passion for fragrances and deep-rooted family traditions in Agarbatti manufacturing, set out to create a company that would blend traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques. From our modest beginnings in a small workshop, SL Agarbatti has expanded its operations across the country, catering to a diverse clientele that appreciates the rich aromas and spiritual significance of our products. Over the years, we have stayed true to our founder's vision of delivering high-quality Agarbatti that enhances rituals, meditation, and everyday moments of tranquillity.

SL Agarbatti, we pride ourselves on our meticulous approach to sourcing raw materials. We select only the finest natural ingredients, ensuring that each Agarbatti stick is infused with authentic fragrances that evoke a sense of peace and serenity. Our manufacturing process combines traditional hand-rolling techniques with advanced machinery to maintain consistency and quality across our product range. Our packaging is designed to minimise environmental impact, reflecting our dedication to preserving the natural beauty of our planet. We are dedicated to enriching the lives of our customers with fragrances that inspire moments of reflection, relaxation, and spiritual connection.
